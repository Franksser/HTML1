Pitbulls

Are they bad dogs?

Of course not!

Pitbulls, and all dogs for that matter, are not born bad or aggressive, a dog's behaviour will always be affected by their owners decisions and actions; making it unfair to judge one dog, or an entire breed thanks to the mistakes that a bad owner made. At the end of the day "All dogs deserve an owner, but not all owners deserve a dog."

How to deal with missbehaving dogs?

The incorrect way: The proper way:
*Excessive phisical and verbal reprehension. *Projecting a calm body language.
*Always rewarding the dog, before, during, or after bad behaviour. *Giving treats when succesfuly meating expectations.
*Leting the dog loose or out of sight around strangers *Keeping an eye on both the dog and any new comers
*Allowing the dog keep socializing with bad behaving dogs. *Noticing when your dog is around a bad influence.

Why should you care?

  • Lower bite rates.

  • Demolish old myths around dog breeds.
  • Decrease uthination/put-down rates.
